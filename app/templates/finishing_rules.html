Understanding Finishing Rules

Keyword Rules

Rules that check for specific keywords in the order description

Example: Adding days for "fold", "crease", etc.

Center Rules

Special rules for specific production centers

Example: Different processing times for certain facilities

Conditions

Additional checks like quantity limits or product types

Example: Extra day for quantities > 10,000
